Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about Fundi Mtaani — tailoring, carpentry, interior design, painting and MTAANI Store, all based in Serem, Vihiga County.

🌎 General — Booking & Location

Where is Fundi Mtaani based?

We are based at Serem Market, Vihiga County, Western Kenya. We serve clients across Serem, Hamisi Sub-County, Vihiga County, and countrywide through our fundi network. WhatsApp 0792405060 to confirm availability in your area.

How do I book any service?

Three easy ways: (1) WhatsApp 0792405060 — send a photo or description, (2) Use our Book Now button, or (3) Google Calendar to pick a slot. WhatsApp is fastest — quote within 30 minutes.

What payment methods do you accept?

M-Pesa (Paybill 522533, Account: FUNDI MTAANI), Cash, Airtel Money, and Bank transfer. A KES 200 deposit secures your booking and is deducted from your final bill.

Do you offer home visits and delivery?

Yes — fundis visit you across Serem and Vihiga County at no extra charge. Delivery within Serem/Vihiga is free. Western Kenya: KES 100–200. Nairobi/Mombasa: KES 300–500. Nationwide via courier: KES 200–500.

How do I track my order?

Use our Order Tracker on the Booking page — enter your WhatsApp number to see live status. You can also WhatsApp us at 0792405060 with your name for an instant update.

What are your working hours?

Monday–Saturday: 8 AM – 7 PM. Sunday: 10 AM – 4 PM. WhatsApp monitored daily including evenings.

✂️ Tailoring & Repairs

How much do repairs cost?

Hemming KES 20–50 · Zipper KES 30–50 · Buttons KES 10–20 · Patching KES 30–50. Tailoring from KES 150 (skirts) to KES 1,000 (suits). Premium: bridal from KES 5,000.

How long does tailoring take?

Quick repairs: same day. Standard tailoring: 24–48 hours. Premium garments: 3–7 days. We give a time estimate when you book and honour it.

Do you supply fabric?

We supply all basic materials (threads, buttons, zippers) at no extra charge. For special fabrics, supply your own or we source them at cost — we always confirm before purchasing anything.

Can I hire clothes for events?

Yes — browse our Hire Clothes tab in MTAANI Store. Premium outfits from KES 300/day. Refundable deposit required. Free pickup at Serem Market.

🛋 Interior Design & Décor

What does a free consultation include?

Our designer visits your home, measures your space, and provides a full mood board and shopping list — completely free, no obligation.

What furniture and décor do you stock?

500+ items across furniture, décor & accessories — sofas (KES 28,000–45,000), beds, wardrobes, curtains, rugs, wall art, lighting and more. 3D rendering available for KES 8,000.

Do you install furniture?

Yes — assembly and installation available for most items. Installation for large pieces (sofas, beds) is often included. Confirm at time of order.

🪓 Carpentry & Furniture

What furniture can you make?

Beds (from KES 12,000), tables (KES 8,500+), wardrobes (KES 22,000–55,000), doors (from KES 8,500), kitchen cabinets, desks and more — all custom-built to your exact measurements from Kenyan hardwood.

How long does custom furniture take?

Standard orders: 7–14 days after deposit. Larger projects may take up to 21 days. We give a delivery date and send WhatsApp updates throughout.

What warranty do you offer?

All carpentry comes with a 5-year craftsmanship warranty. Any joint, finish, or structural failure under normal use is repaired or replaced at no cost.

🎨 Painting Services

What painting do you offer?

Interior rooms (from KES 3,500), exterior walls (from KES 5,000), feature walls (from KES 2,500), full house interiors (from KES 18,000), ceilings, doors, textured finishes, and free colour consultation.

Do you supply paint?

Yes — all paints and materials are included in the quoted price. We use quality interior emulsion and weatherproof exterior paints. Colour consultation is always free.

Is there a painting guarantee?

Exterior painting: 2-year weather guarantee. Interior: 12-month satisfaction guarantee. Any peeling or cracking due to workmanship is fixed free of charge.

🛍 MTAANI Store & Online Orders

How do I order from the store?

Browse the store online, add to cart and checkout. Or WhatsApp 0792405060 with item name, size and delivery address — we confirm within 30 minutes.

Can I get free alterations on items I buy?

Yes — all ready-made clothing comes with one free alteration. Provide your measurements at checkout and our tailors will adjust before dispatch.

How long does delivery take?

Serem/Vihiga: same/next day, free. Western Kenya: 1–2 days, KES 100–200. Nairobi/Mombasa: 2–3 days, KES 300–500. Nationwide: 3–5 days via courier.

⭐ Quality & Guarantee

What if I'm not satisfied?

100% satisfaction guarantee on all services. We redo work free within 7 days. Tailoring includes one free fitting adjustment. If still unsatisfied, deposit is fully refunded.

How are fundis verified?

Skills assessment, background check, reference verification and ongoing rating monitoring. Only fundis with 4.0+ ratings stay on our platform.

Can I request the same fundi again?

Yes — mention the fundi's name when booking. Gold and Platinum loyalty members get a dedicated named fundi for all future bookings.
